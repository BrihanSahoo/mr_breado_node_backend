# V61 Outlet Business Consistency

- Internal outlet accounts bypass marketplace restaurant verification.
- Outlet login accepts admin-created username, email, or phone credentials.
- Outlet managers cannot create/delete foods; they see only admin-assigned foods.
- Outlet managers submit daily stock to head office.
- Admin outlet creation includes GSTIN and invoice identity.
- Missing GSTIN is highlighted in outlet dashboard and can be edited.
- Outlet orders remain isolated by outlet id; head office sees all outlet orders.
- Outlet business audit exposes customer, items, totals, stock submissions, and outlet invoice identity.
- Razorpay v22/v26 create-order flow is unchanged.
